About this website

A personal project website that displays football statistics! Has information on the top 5 European leagues with detailed statistics on both the teams and the players.

Features

- In depth statistics and visuals on teams and players within the top 5 European football leagues.
- For each team, analysis on their relative strengths and weaknesses are provided with recommended players to sign, prioritizing their weaknesses first.
- User registration and login to follow your favorite teams and players and be given pertinent information on trasnfer rumors regarding them.

Planned Features

- More comprehensive implementation and use of information from the prior season
- A system of updating the results weekly and keeping of with the new season as it unfolds
- Possible activity of predicting game result.
- More robust player recommender (more in depth MF options, better DF criteria)
- Twitter tweet scanning to include more accounts/more comprehensive. Currently big teams and big players are the most tweeted about.
- Make tweepy faster/not as slow on startup
- Create a player analysis like the one team has, identify areas the player could improve on
