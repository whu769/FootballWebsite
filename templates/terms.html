Terminology

M/MP Matches Played
W Matches Won
D Matches Drawn
L Matches Lost
PTS Points
GF Goals Forward (Goals a team scored)
GA Goals Allowed (Goals a team let in)
xG Expected goals scored
xGA Expected Goals Allowed
Position Area the player plays in (FW - Forward, MF - Midfielder, DF - Defender, GK - Goalkeeper)
xA Expected Assists
NPxG Expected non-penalty goals
Pos% Percentage of ball possession a team has on average
GP90 Average Goals a Team Scores Per 90 (Game)
xGP90 Expected Average Goals a Team Scores Per 90
SoTP90 Average Number of Shots on Target Per 90 (in a game)
GPSoT Ratio of Goals Per Shot on Target
ShP90 Average Number of Shots Per 90 (On and Off target)
GlsP90 Average Goals Scored every 90 minutes
ClrP90 Number of Clearances Per 90 Minutes (Removing the ball from dangerous areas in which the opponent can score)
BlkP90 Number of Blocked Shots Per 90 Minutes
TklIntP90 Number of Tackles and Interceptions Per 90 Minutes
Tackles P90 Number of Tackles Per 90 Minutes
Successful Tackles P90 Number of Successful Tackles Per 90
Interceptions P90 Number of Interceptions Per 90 (Player 'intercepts' a pass from the opposition and obtains possession)
PassP Overall Percentage of Successful Passes
LPP Percentage of Successful Long Passes
MPP Percentage of Successful Medium Passes
SPP Percentage of Short Passes
SP90 Number of Short Passes Played Per 90
MP90 Number of Medium Passes Played Per 90
LP90 Number of Long Passes Played Per 90
FTP90 Number of Passes Played in the Opposition's Final Third Per 90
KP90 Number of Key Passes Played Per 90
GAP90 (Goalkeeping) Average Number of Goals a Goalie Concedes Per 90 Minutes
PSxGP90 PSxG - Post-Shot Expected Goals , The number of Expected Goals a Goalkeeper will concede per 90
PSxG_dP90 Post Shot Expected Goals Minus the Actual Goals Allowed divided by 90 minutes, positive numbers means the goalkeeper is performing above expectations
Cross Stop % Percentage of Crosses Stopped (by the Goalkeeper only)
AvgDist Goalkeeper's Average Distance Away From Goal
Out of Pen Box Actions P90 Average Number of Actions (passes, clearances, blocks, etc) a goalkeeper makes outside of his penalty box per 90
